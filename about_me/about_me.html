Jobs Description

I first started working at the age of 16 whilst living in Darwin, NT. Being already interested in computers I started in one of the most common jobs for a teenager, fast food to assist in buying my own computer equipment. Over the next few years I moved from NT and started focusing more on my education. Once I finished Year 12 and starteed my Diploma in IT i once again started to work to assist in paying bills and following my interests. I started working for a family owned companie of California Dry Cleaners in Brisbane.

Part way through 2015 I received my first break into the computer industry as I was chosen out of hundreds of applicants to take part in a Hewlett Packard Initiative in Brisbane to learn and work as a Test Analyst. During my time working for HP I had the opportunity to work on the test versions of the new Medicare systems to ensure that the system was working \ within parameters and had no issues through the analysis of multiple Test Cases and Bug Analysis. After 6 months with the HP program the organisers had to let some of the staff go as it was designed primarily as a training program and as such I was one of the people that they let go. From here I picked up work at the YMCA at Joyner.

At the start of this year, through a work awareness company I was able to gain volutary employment at one of the local computer shops known as Ed's PC. While I was at Ed's PC I was tasked with computer repairs, generating repair reports, correspondace with customers and couriers, general customer assistance and diagnostics on computer equipment.
